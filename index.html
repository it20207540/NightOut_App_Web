What made we do this

Research problem

The pervasive use of social media has shifted the focus from real-life gatherings to virtual interactions, leaving physical meetups and social gatherings overlooked. This trend is particularly challenging for individuals who relocate to new environments, as they often struggle to adapt to their new surroundings, resulting in feelings of isolation and loneliness. These emotions can have severe implications for mental health, including increased stress, heightened risk of depression, social anxiety, and related issues. Furthermore, social media has worsened these challenges for many.

Furthermore, existing market applications do not adequately address this problem, leading to business failures caused by investments made without access to accurate statistical data. Businesses often struggle to identify their target audience's preferences, leading to misguided strategies and missed opportunities. As a result, there is a pressing need for a comprehensive solution that caters to both individual well-being and the data-driven needs of businesses in today's evolving digital landscape. This necessitates a holistic solution to bridge the gap effectively.

User Evaluation

R.N.Akmeemana

In a mobile app-driven era, personalized user experiences are crucial. Our proposal introduces a mobile app component that employs machine learning for user behavior analysis, offering personalized recommendations. This approach enhances user experiences by understanding preferences, interests, and behavior patterns through data analysis, including browsing history and search queries.

The component will employ machine learning algorithms, including TF-IDF Vectorization and cosine similarity, to create highly personalized recommendations. To ensure user privacy, we'll implement robust data protection measures, including anonymization and secure data storage. The success of this initiative hinges on the quality and efficiency of machine learning models, the careful balance between personalization and privacy, and the ability to gather and analyze data at scale.

By offering tailored recommendations, this mobile application component not only enhances user engagement and loyalty but also helps users discover new content and products they are likely to enjoy. As the mobile app landscape continues to evolve, leveraging machine learning for personalization is essential for developers seeking to remain competitive and provide exceptional user experiences.

Socialization

K.H.N.D. Dharmapala

In response to the challenges of subjective and untrustworthy user ratings on social media profiles and the difficulty of managing the vast volume of social media data, our system leverages advanced machine learning algorithms. These algorithms, such as collaborative filtering and KNN, enable us to provide objective, data-driven evaluations of social media profiles.

Users can customize their evaluation criteria, and verified users can contribute ratings, which are showcased on a dedicated explore page, ensuring trustworthiness.

Furthermore, our system utilizes user behavior analysis and community recommendations to deliver tailored event suggestions, enhancing the social media experience. It also features chat platforms for real-time interactions between users attending the same event.

Our innovative approach benefits social media marketers, influencers, and businesses, enabling data-driven decision-making based on transparent profile evaluations. This system promises to transform the landscape of social media management and user engagement.

Profit Maximization

P.T. Samarasekara

In the competitive event management industry, our focus is on maximizing profits through a personalized recommendation system, incorporating a Profit Maximization component that employs advanced machine learning techniques like Multiple Linear Regression and Decision Tree Classification.

This system tailors recommendations to individual user preferences, promoting engagement and community participation. The integration of gamification elements further enhances user involvement. Continuous assessment and adaptation are key to maintaining profitability.

To develop the Profit Maximization component, tasks include business registration, predictive algorithms, real-time dashboard visualization, and data collection for user behavior analysis. The objective is to empower businesses with data-driven insights, enabling them to thrive in the event management industry and make informed decisions to achieve lasting success.
